LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT
That I, RUTH WATKINS EVANS, formerly of Taylor, in the County of Lackawanna and State of Pennsylvania, being of sound mind, memory and understanding do make and publish this my last Will and Testament, hereby revoking and making void all former Wills by me at any time heretofore made.
AND FIRST, I direct that my funeral be conducted in a manner corresponding with my estate and situation in life and that all my legally enforceable debts and funeral expenses be fully paid and satisfied as soon as conveniently may be after my decease.
AND to such estate as it has pleased God to entrust I dispose of the same as follows, viz:
SECOND: I give, devise and bequeath all of my estate, whether the same be real, personal or mixed, of whatever kind, whatever nature and wheresoever situate at the time of my death, to my beloved son, WILLIAM EVANS, his heirs and assigns, absolutely and forever.
