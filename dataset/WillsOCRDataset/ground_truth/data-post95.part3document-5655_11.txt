THE LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT OF EUGENE J. EMMONS, WIDOWER
BE IT REMEMBERED THAT I, EUGENE J. EMMONS, WIDOWER, of 24-26 Cemetery Street, Carbondale, Lackawanna County, Pennsylvania, being of sound and disposing mind, memory and understanding, do make, publish and declare the following to be my Last Will and Testament, hereby revoking and making null and void any and all Wills or writings in the nature thereof by me at any time heretofore made.
ITEM ONE: I direct that all my just debts and funeral expenses shall be paid as conveniently may be after my decease.
ITEM TWO: All of the rest, residue and remainder of my estate, of whatever nature and kind, whether real or personal and wheresoever situate, I give, devise and bequeath to my son, STEVEN E. EMMONS, of 6131 Cool Springs, Gainesville, Georgia 30506, to be his forever.
ITEM SIX: It is my wish and I so direct that BENJAMIN S. SCHNESSEL, ESQUIRE, of Carbondale, Pennsylvania, serve as Attorney for my Estate.
