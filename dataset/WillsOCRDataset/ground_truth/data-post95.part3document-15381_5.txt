LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT
I, JAMES J. FAIRCLOUGH, presently of 1719 Jackson Street, Scranton, Lackawa nna County, Pennsylvania, declare this to be my last Will and Testament hereby revoking any and all wills or writings in the nature thereof by me at any time heretofore made.
FIRST: I direct my Executors hereinafter named to pay my just debts, funeral expenses and taxes of whatever sort or nature as soon as may be after my decease.
SECOND: All the rest, residue and remainder of my estate, I give, devise and bequeath to my mother, Mary Fairclough; provided, however, that if she should not survive me, said estate, in equal shares, to my sister, Jean (Fairclough) Haffner, presently of 1048 Meade Avenue, Scranton, Pennsylvania, and Robert P. Fairclough, presently of 441 Tenth Avenue, Scranton, Pennsylvania.
THIRD: I hereby nominate, constitute and appoint Jean Haffner and Robert P. Fairclough as Executors of my estate.
IN WITNESS WHERE OF, I, JAMES J. FAIRCLOUGH, the Testator have to this, my Will, set my hand and seal this 14th day of March, One Thousand Nine Hundred and Eighty-one.
