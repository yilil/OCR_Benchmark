Last Will and Testament of ADELINE ENGEL
I, ADELINE ENGEL, do make, publish and declare this to be my Last Will and Testament.
I. I hereby revoke all previous wills, codicils and testamentary writings made by me.
II. I direct that all my just debts, funeral and burial expenses, and all taxes whatsoever the same may be, shall be paid as soon as may be convenient.
III. I give and bequeath all of my estate to my sister, SARAH A. ENGEL.
IV. In the event my sister, SARAH A. ENGEL, is not living at the time of my death, then I give, devise and bequeath my estate as follows:
(a) To my niece, JUDITH PERCIVAL, all of my real estate;
(b) To MRS. GLENN GALLAGHER, One Thousand ($1,000.00) Dollars;
(c) To DAVID PERCIVAL, One Thousand ($1,000.00) Dollars;
