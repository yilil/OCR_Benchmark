Last Will and Testament of BETTY B. EVANKAVITCH
I, BETTY B. EVANKAVITCH of the Borough of Archbald, County of Lackawanna and State of Pennsylvania, being of sound mind and memory, do hereby make, publish and declare this to be my Last Will and Testament, hereby revoking any and all Wills and/or Codicils made by me heretofore.
ARTICLE ONE: I direct that all my just debts and funeral expenses be paid by my Executor hereinafter appointed, as soon as convenient in the administration of my Estate. I further direct that my funeral be conducted in a modest manner in keeping with my Estate and position in life.
ARTICLE TWO: (a) I give, devise and bequeath all of my property, be it real, personal and/or mixed, of whatever nature and wheresoever situate, which I may own or have the right to dispose of at the time of my death equally to my sons, JOSEPH C. EVANKAVITCH JR. and RICHARD D. EVANKAVITCH, their heirs and assigns forever.
ARTICLE THREE: I direct that my Executor hereinafter named shall not be required to give bond for the faithful performance of the duties as Executor in this or any jurisdiction.
ARTICLE FOUR: I hereby nominate, constitute and appoint my son, JOSEPH C. EVANKAVITCH, JR., executor of this my Last Will and Testament. In the event my son, JOSEPH C. EVANKAVITCH, JR., predeceases me or is unable for any reason whatsoever to serve as the Executor of this my Last Will and Testament, I hereby nominate, constitute and appoint my son., RICHARD D. EVANKAVITCH, to serve as the Executor of this my Last Will and Testament.
