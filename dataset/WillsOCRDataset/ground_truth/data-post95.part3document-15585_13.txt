LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT
ANN FALAT
I, ANN FALAT, of the City of Scranton, Lackawanna County, Pennsylvania, declare this my last Will and revoke any Will previously made by me.
ITEM I: I direct that all my just debts and funeral expenses and all expenses of my last illness, shall be paid from my residuary estate as soon as practicable after my decease as a part of the expense of the administration of my estate.
ITEM II: I devise and bequeath all of my Estate of every nature and wherever situate to my sister, Alice Kurylo, and my sister, Catherine Demkowicz. Should either my sister, Alice Kurylo, or my sister, Catherine Demkowicz, predecease me, her share shall be added to the share for my other sister. Should both my sister, Alice Kurylo, and my sister, Catherine Demkowicz, predecease me, I devise and bequeath all of my Estate of every nature and wherever situate as follows:
A. one-third thereof to my sister-in-law, Pauline Martini.
B. one-third thereof to my niece, Joan Thomson.
C. on-third thereof to niece, Catherine Chiaporelli.
ITEM III: I direct that all taxes that may be assessed in consequence of my death, of whatever nature and by whatever jurisdiction imposed, shall be paid from my residuary estate as a part of the expense of the administration of my estate.
ITEM IV: I appoint my sister, Alice Kurylo, Executrix of this my Last Will. Should my sister, Alice Kurylo, fail to qualify or cease to act as Executrix, I appoint my sister, Catherine Demkowicz, Executrix of this my Last Will.
ITEM VII: I direct that my Executrix or her successor(s) shall not be required to give bond for the faithful performance of their duties in any jurisdiction.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand this 3rd day of July, 1996
