LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT
I, MARGARET H. EVANS, presently of 281 Short Hill Drive, Clarks Green, Lackawanna County, Pennsylvania, do make this my last Will and Testament hereby revoking any and all wills or writings in the nature thereof by me at any time heretofore made.
FIRST: I direct my Executrix hereinafter named to pay my just debts, funeral expenses and taxes of whatever sort or nature as soon as may be after my decease.
SECOND: I give and bequeath to Clarks Green United Methodist Church of Clarks Green, Pennsylvania, the sum of One Thousand ($1,000.00) Dollars.
THIRD: All the rest, residue and remainder of my estate, I give, devise and bequeath to my daughter, CAROL E. JAMES, pre-sently of Box 158, 346 Main Street, Dalton, Pennsylvania 18414.
FOURTH: I nominate, constitute and appoint CAROL E. JAMES as Executrix of my estate, and direct that she shall not be required to give bond for the faithful performance of her duties in any jurisdiction.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I, MARGARET H. EVANS the Testatrix, have to this, my Will, set my hand and seal this 12th day of May, One Thousand Nine Hundred and Eighty-two.
