ALL THE REST, RESIDUE AND REMAINDER of my estate, whether the same be real, personal or mixed, of whatever kind, whatever nature and wheresoever situate at the time of my death, I give, devise and bequeath to my beloved son, WILLIAM EVANS, his heirs and assigns, absolutely and forever.
AND I hereby nominate, constitute and appoint my beloved son, WILLIAM EVANS, Executor, of this my last Will and Testament and desire he serve without bond.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I, RUTH WATKINS EVANS, the Testatrix, have to this, my Will written on two sheets of paper, set my hand and seal, this 21 day of March A. D. Two Thousand and Two (2002).
