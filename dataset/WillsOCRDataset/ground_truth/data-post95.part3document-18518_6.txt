Be It Remembered
That I, JOSEPH T. FARRELL of SCRANTON in the County of LACKAWANNA and State of Pennsylvania, being of sound mind, memory and understanding do make and publish this my last Will and Testament, hereby revoking and making void all former Wills by me at any time heretofore made.
And First, I direct that my funeral be conducted in a manner corresponding with my estate and situation in life and that all my just debts and funeral expen-ses be fully paid and satisfied as soon as conveniently may be after my decease.
And to such estate as it has pleased God to entrust to me, I dispose of the same as follows, viz;
ITEM: I give, devise and bequeath all of my estate of whatever nature and wheresoever the same may be situate at the time of my death to my beloved wife, JULIA FARRELL.
ITEM: In the event that my beloved wife, JULIA FARRELL, does not survive me, then, in that event, I give, devise and bequeath Fifty (50%) Percent of my estate to ELIZABETH "BETTY" BESTRYCKI. remaining Fifty (50%) Percent of my estate shall be divided equally among, MARY LEBO, MILDRED MCNAMMERA and FRANK FARRELL in equal shares, share and share alike.
