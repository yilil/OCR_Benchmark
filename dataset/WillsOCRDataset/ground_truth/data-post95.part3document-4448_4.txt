LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT
I RONALD ELKO, a married man, residing at 92 Valentine Street in the town of Moosic Penna., declare this to be my LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT.
FIRST: I hereby revoke any and all former wills and codicils at any time theretofore made by me.
SECOND: I direct that my just debts and funeral and administration expenses be paid by Executrix as soon after my death as may be practicable.
THIRD: I give, devise, and bequeath all my property, both real and personal, to my wife Maryalice Elko.
If my wife should predecease me, I give, devise and bequeath all my property to my daughter, Kimberly Ann Elko, to all shares, per stirpes.
FOURTH: I nominate and appoint my wife Maryalice Elko to be Executrix of this my last will. If she should predecease me fail to qualify, or cease to act as such, I appoint Kimberly Ann Elko as Executrix in her place and stead. I direct that neither shall be required to file any bond in any jurisdiction.
